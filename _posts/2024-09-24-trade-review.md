## Failures
1. Took an unplanned trade earlier in the day with too much risk and lost.
	- Solution: Always trade according to plan. Do not break the rules. Only if it is planned then take the entry. I should've waited until the plan is clearly written down before trading another candle apart from the 1600 candle.

2. Took a heavy bet and opened four 0.5 lots with 100 pip SL to try recover losses from earlier.
	- Solution: Even if we win, we must maintain the risk. We will win back equity slowly. No need to win it back in one trade.

3. Kept moving SL to stay in the trade. 
	- Solution: With the new CRT model from Hancock, we will move away from ATR(4) and use a set SL based on CSD levels. ATR(4) can be used as a confluence for safe volatility levels.

4. Opened a trade at an unplanned CRT candle.
	- Solution: Hancock's CRT model now gives us 4 possible H4 candles to watch: 0400, 0800, 1600, and 2000. This gives us more options. They are the 11AM, 3PM ,11PM, 3AM H4 candles. Confirmation comes from a purge of CR high/low followed by CSD1 and CSD2 on M5 or M1. I will post about this strategy.
