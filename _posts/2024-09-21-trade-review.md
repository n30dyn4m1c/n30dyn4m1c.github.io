- Retracement started up to 3AM. The positions went into profit slightly. I did not close, hoping they would go lower.
- I was watching stuff on YouTube into the early morning so I decided to keep positions open while I watched. 
- Price bounced in the zone twice. CRH was acting like a support now.
- Lowered SL to less than $500 total risk.

of $193.30

## Successes

## Failures
1. Didn't invalidate CRT trade setup after purge timing lapsed. Kept watching after 12:30AM PGT even into 1:30AM PGT where I entered. This was outside of the time window.
- Solution: Invalidate the trade setup after 12:30AM PGT if there is no TS. Do not take a CRT trade. Leave it.
  
2. Didn't wait for a proper turtle soup on M15. I entered off M1.
- Solution: Do not watch M1. Watch at M5 and M15 primarily. Even if M1 forms a TS, do not enter, wait for M5 to form a TS. Also, wait for TS pattern. Let it come to you. If it doesn't come, don't force it.

3. Do not hope in trading if you are wrong. I hoped London close would bring a reversal.
- Solution: It is better to be straight forward if there is no setup. Take the loss. Admit your loss and your mistake. Don't hope the market will turn and make you right. You are wrong. Accept it.

4. Didn't pray and commit the trades to God and revisit the word foundations of trading.
- Solution: Always give a minimum of 10 minutes to focus to pray before getting into analyses and setup. Learn to be thankful, to be submitted to God, and to give Him all the glory as well. This will bring tremendous peace of mind and will make trading less stressful.
